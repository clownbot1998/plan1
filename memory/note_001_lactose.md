**The Thing About Josh and Matt**

Tyler noticed it first with the coffee.

Matt always made two cups. Always. Even when Josh was still asleep, even when Josh had said the night before *I'm not doing coffee tomorrow, I'm switching to tea* — Matt made two cups. And when Josh shuffled out of his room at 9am, hair wrecked, mumbling about tea, the cup was already there and Josh picked it up without looking and drank it and that was that.

Tyler mentioned this to Zac on the fire escape.

"I've known for six months," Zac said, not looking up from his phone.

"Six — how?"

"Movie night. March. Josh picked the movie. Matt said it was a bad choice. Josh said fine, you pick then. Matt said no, yours is fine. They spent twenty minutes doing that. You fell asleep."

"That's not —"

"Josh picked the blanket up off the floor and put it on Matt without breaking eye contact with the screen." Zac finally looked up. "Without. Breaking. Eye contact."

Tyler stared out at the street below.

"So what do we do?" he said.

Zac shrugged. "I've been waiting for them to figure it out. I've got twelve bucks on November."

"That's six months away."

"I know." Zac stood up, slid the window open. "You want in?"

From inside, they could hear Josh laughing at something Matt said. Then Matt laughing, a beat later, like he'd surprised himself.

Tyler put his hand out. "Twenty. July."

Zac shook it without hesitating.
